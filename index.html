Profile

about me

   Myself Dr. P. Victer Paul, currently working as Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Information Technology Kottayam, Kerala, India, an Institute of National Importance of India. I completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Computer Science (2015) from Pondicherry Central University, Pondicherry, India.

   I completed my B.Tech. in Information Technology (2007) from SMVEC and M.Tech. in Network and Internet Engineering (2011) from Pondicherry Central University, Pondicherry, India. I am a recipient of eminent INSPIRE fellowship from Department of Science and Technology, New Delhi.

    I secured a University Gold medal and University Rank holder in M.Tech and B.Tech studies respectively. I also received “TCS Best Student Award 2010” from TCS for academic excellence at Pondicherry Central University, Puducherry. I have 8+ years of academic & industrial experience and published over 90 research articles in various International Journals & Conferences.

   Currently, I am working in the fields of Data Analytics, Machine Learning, Optimization algorithms and Cloud Computing.
